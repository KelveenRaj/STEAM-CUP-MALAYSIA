About Us

STEAM CUP MALAYSIA is an annual robotics competition organized by Robotclub® Malaysia since 2012. STEAM CUP MALAYSIA is opened to members of e@Robotclub® throughout Malaysia, as a platform for all members to embark on new challenges, gain new experience, skills enhancement and enjoyment. The rules for each category will be revised every year, as we aim to increase the student's competency skills from time to time.

e@Robotclub® is teaching STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics) through the use of robotic kits, our objective is to increase the students’ interest in exploring their engineering knowledge through practical courses. All our students are using quality robotics products "Made in Korea" and undergoing the robotics courses at e@Robotclub® Authorized Robotics Learning centers.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why is SteamCup Malaysia 2021 being held online?

    This years SteamCup Malaysia competition is held online due to the covid-19 SOP restrictions set by the government and to eliminate the risk of covid-19 among participants.

  • How do you choose the winner for each category?

    Those participants that have the highest number of views will go into the final round where they will be evaluated by our judges to choose the winner for each category.

  • When will you announce the winners?

    The winners for SteamCup MCO will be revealed on 15 July, The winners list will be posted on our Facebook page.

  • Will the competition be held online next year aswell?

    We hope to bring back SteamCup Malaysia to a physical event in 2022, but that depends on the covid-19 pandemic status in Malaysia, more information on this will be posted soon on our Facebook page.
